Corporate Overview<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Scalehigh Technologies is an IT services, consulting and business solutions organization partnering many of the world\u2019s largest businesses in their transformational journeys for the last 50 years. It has a global presence, deep domain expertise in multiple industry verticals and a complete portfolio of offerings \u2013 grouped under consulting and service integration, digital transformation services, cloud services, cognitive business operations, and products and platforms \u2013 targeting every C-suite stakeholder.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The Company leverages all these and its deep contextual knowledge of its customers\u2019 businesses to craft unique, high quality, high impact solutions designed to deliver differentiated business outcomes. These solutions are delivered using its Secure Borderless Workspaces\u2122 (SBWS\u2122) operating model which enables a highly distributed, Location Independent Agile\u2122 delivery.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

TCS geographic footprint covers North America, Latin America, the United Kingdom, Continental Europe, Asia-Pacific, India, and Middle-East and Africa.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

TCS considers industry verticals as its go-to-market business segments. The five key vertical clusters are: Banking, Financial Services and Insurance (BFSI), Retail and Consumer Business, Communications, Media and Technology (CMT), Manufacturing and Others. The last category includes Life Sciences and Healthcare, Energy, Resources and Utilities, Public Services and others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
